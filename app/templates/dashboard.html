📊 Match Scoring Formula

Score = (40% Skills + 25% Title + 20% Seniority + 15% Location) × (1 - Exclusion) × LLM

Components:
  • Skills (40%): Overlap between job requirements and CV skills
  • Title (25%): Job title alignment with CV experience
  • Seniority (20%): Level fit (intern → C-level)
  • Location (15%): Remote/location compatibility
  • Exclusion: Blocked keywords = 0 score
  • LLM: Optional AI adjustment (0.5-1.5x)
